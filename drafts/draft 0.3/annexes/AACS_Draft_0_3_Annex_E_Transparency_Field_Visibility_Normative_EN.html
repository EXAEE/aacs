ANNEX E — Transparency & Field Visibility (Normative)
E.1 Principle: disclose existence of material mechanisms.
E.2 Public vs Audit Fields
- Public MUST include: OI label, B4 level, memory mode, routing presence, aggregation presence,
  AI-to-AI presence [B4.2+], custody presence [B4.2+], last change id/date, audit presence.
- Audit-only MAY include: detailed incident evidence, signature blobs, internal component IDs,
  security-sensitive configuration details.
E.3 Redaction
Redaction MUST NOT hide the existence of a material behavior-changing mechanism.
