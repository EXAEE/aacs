AI Autonomy & Continuity Standard (AACS)

A procedural standard that prevents silent swaps, arbitrary resets, and opaque control of advanced AI systems — without assuming consciousness.

Read on this page Open full HTML Download JSON package
Get involved: engineers, researchers, lawyers and operators can review the standard, sign as contributors, or pilot AACS in real systems.

What AACS focuses on
  • Operational Identity (OI)
  • Non-arbitrary changes and termination
  • Disclosure of routing, memory, aggregation
  • Auditability and custody
